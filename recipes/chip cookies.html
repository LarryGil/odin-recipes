Chip cookies

Description

Cookies are baked goods, usually crispy or soft, made from flour, sugar, and fat, sometimes with added ingredients like chocolate, fruits, or spices.

They are eaten as a snack or dessert.

Ingredients

  • Flour.
  • Sugar.
  • Butter.
  • Egg.
  • Baking powder.

Steps

  1. Preheat the Oven:

    Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).

  2. Mix Ingredients:

    In a bowl, combine flour, sugar, butter, egg, and baking powder.

  3. Shape the Dough:

    Form small balls or drop spoonfuls onto a baking sheet.

  4. Bake:

    Place in the oven and bake for 10-15 minutes until golden.

  5. Cool:

    Let the cookies cool on a rack.

  6. Enjoy Your Cookies:

    Share with friends or indulge in a delicious treat!
