Hamburguer

Description

A hamburger is a sandwich made of cooked ground meat served between two buns, often topped with lettuce, tomato, onion, cheese, and sauces.

They're commonly enjoyed at barbecues, fast-food restaurants, and casual dining spots.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef.
  • Hamburger bun.
  • Salt.
  • Pepper.
  • Toppings: lettuce, tomato, onion, cheese, ketchup, mustard.

Steps

  1. Prepare the patty:

    Form the ground beef into a round patty and season with salt and pepper.

  2. Cook the patty:

    Grill or pan-fry the patty over medium heat for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until cooked to your liking.

  3. Toast the bun:

    Lightly toast the hamburger bun on the grill or in a toaster.

  4. Assemble the burger:

    Place the cooked patty on the bottom half of the bun. Add your desired toppings (lettuce, tomato, onion, cheese).

  5. Add sauces:

    Spread ketchup and mustard on the top half of the bun, if desired.

  6. Serve:

    Place the top half of the bun on the burger, cut in half if you like.

  7. Enjoy:

    Enjoy your homemade hamburger!
