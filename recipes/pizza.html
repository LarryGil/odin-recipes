Pizza

Description

The pizza is an Italian dish.

It is very popular and can be customized in different ways.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough.
  • Tomato sauce.
  • Mozzarella cheese.
  • Pepperoni.

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough:

    Mix 2 cups of flour, 1 teaspoon of salt, 1 teaspoon of sugar, and 1 teaspoon of dry yeast in a bowl.

    Add 3/4 cup of warm water and 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Mix until a dough forms.

    Knead the dough for about 5-7 minutes until smooth. Let it rest for 30 minutes.

  2. Preheat the Oven:

    Preheat your oven to 220 °C (428 °F).

  3. Roll Out the Dough:

    On a floured surface, roll out the dough with a rolling pin until it reaches your desired size (about 12 inches in diameter).

  4. Add the Sauce:

    Spread a layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving about a 1 cm border.

  5. Add the Cheese:

    Place a generous amount of mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.

  6. Add the Topping:

    Add your choice of toppings, such as pepperoni.

  7. Bake the Pizza:

    Place the pizza on a baking sheet or pizza stone and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.

  8. Serve:

    Remove the pizza from the oven, let it cool slightly, slice it, and serve.

  9. Enjoy:

    Enjoy your homemade pizza!
